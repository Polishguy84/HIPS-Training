About HIPS Consulting

HIPS Consulting, also known as Hiding in Plain Sight, specializes in expert surveillance training tailored for law enforcement professionals seeking to master the art of covert operations. With a focus on mobile surveillance techniques, HIPS offers comprehensive programs that blend real-world scenarios, advanced technology integration, and urban stealth tactics to ensure officers can track suspects undetected in any environment. Led by experienced instructors Sgt. Dave Chambers and Sgt. Brent Sitowski, HIPS provides hands-on training to enhance tactical skills, leveraging tools like GPS trackers, drones, and encrypted communications. For more information on upcoming sessions, such as the May 14–16, 2025, training in Medford, Oregon, contact HIPS at hipsconsultingllc@gmail.com, or reach out directly to Sgt. Chambers at 541-892-7888 or Sgt. Sitowski at 541-301-4096.
